OUR FACILITIES

We provide the maximum facilities to maintain the comfotablity among the students

CAFETERIA

We are running two cafeterias under our management. You can get tasty and healthy foods here. Usually they provide the foods according the food orders. Also you can buy some stationery things here.

PLAY GROUNDS

We have two play grounds with the atheletic trcks here. We have the ground facility for Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Netball, Elle, Korfball and Hockey. Also we have indoor stadiums for Badminton, Karate, Chess, Carrom and Table Tennis. Students who are interested in sport can intract with our paid coaches

GYMNASIUM

We have a Gymnasium with enough equipments. This is running as free for the student's. Students who wants to use the gym should register every month. Also we conducting awarness programs once a month. Students who are interested in gym can get knowledge from our paid coach.

Hostels

In srilankan universities here only students can get the hostel facilities in their whole academic period. There are some rules and regulations in the hostels. Our hostel wardens will provide the rules and regulation for all the students.

LIBRARY

We are have a great library here. Students should register with your register book for library usage. Also students can access our smart library facility.
